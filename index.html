Introduction

Welcome to Smart Salah ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our Smart Salah mobile application (the "App"). By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the terms described herein. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

📋

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials.
  • User Preferences: Prayer notification settings, preferred calculation method, Do Not Disturb preferences, and other customization options.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your device's geographic location to calculate accurate prayer times. This data is processed locally and is not stored on our servers.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and general diagnostic data.
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, features accessed, and interaction logs to help us improve the experience.

Notifications & Permissions

  • Notification Access: To send prayer time reminders and alerts.
  • Do Not Disturb Access: To automatically enable DND mode during prayer times, if enabled.
  • Location Access: To determine accurate prayer times based on your geographic position.
⚙️

How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate and display accurate prayer times based on your location.
  • To send prayer time notifications and reminders.
  • To enable Do Not Disturb functionality during prayer times.
  • To personalize your App experience and remember your preferences.
  • To improve, maintain, and optimize our App and services.
  • To communicate with you regarding updates, support, or service-related announcements.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or security concerns.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party providers who assist us in operating the App, subject to strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, or that of our users or the public.

Data Storage & Security

We implement appropriate administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Age Eligibility

Smart Salah is suitable for users aged 10 years and above. The App is a simple prayer utility that helps users keep track of Salah times and automatically silence their phone during prayers. It does not contain any content requiring parental guidance.

No parental consent is required to use this App, as it does not collect sensitive personal information from minors and its functionality is limited to prayer time notifications and device silent mode management.
