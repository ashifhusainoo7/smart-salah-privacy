Introduction

Welcome to Smart Salah ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our Smart Salah mobile application (the "App"). By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the terms described herein. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

📋

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials.
  • User Preferences: Prayer notification settings, preferred calculation method, Do Not Disturb preferences, and other customization options you configure within the App.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your device's geographic location to calculate accurate prayer times. This data is processed locally on your device and is not stored on our servers.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and general diagnostic data.
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, features accessed, and interaction logs to help us improve the App experience.

Notifications & Permissions

  • Notification Access: To send prayer time reminders and alerts.
  • Do Not Disturb Access: To automatically enable DND mode during prayer times, if you enable this feature.
  • Location Access: To determine accurate prayer times based on your geographic position.
⚙️

How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate and display accurate prayer times based on your location.
  • To send prayer time notifications and reminders.
  • To enable Do Not Disturb functionality during prayer times.
  • To personalize your App experience and remember your preferences.
  • To improve, maintain, and optimize our App and services.
  • To communicate with you regarding updates, support, or service-related announcements.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or security concerns.
